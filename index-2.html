About Us

This is a non-academic programme that offers valuable and practical opportunities for professional development through selected Government agencies, conferences, networking and practical work experiences.

Over the 12-week program, interns follow a well-planned program where placements will be made to relevant departments and additional learning experience will be provided at workshops and seminars.

ABOUT US

Nationality

Be a Nigerian national who has completed the National Youth Service Corp Programme;

Certification

Have a Bachelor’s degree in any discipline from a recognized university

Language/Age Range

Have an excellent command of English, written and oral; Be between the ages of 21-26 by the closing of the application period;

IT Skills

Be proficient in computer skills; and Be energetic, self-starting and eager to tackle new ideas and projects.
